Demonstration of using 'empty' EHR accounts

This is a demonstration of a select box in an EHR that is used for assignments of tasks.
 
The accounts preceded by the asterisks in the demonstration below are empty accounts
  • No user has credentials for authentication to the asterisked accounts.
  • The tasks are completed by a pool of billers, front desk staff, nurses, etc who browse the inboxes of the empty accounts.
  • These staff members who browse the inboxes for tasks to complete are logged in with their own, individual credentials that are unique
  • The actions by the pools of staff are logged under the specific usernames of the individual staff members.
 
Does this demonstration, if implemented, comply with: § 164.312(a)(2)(i) to “assign a unique name and/or number for identifying and tracking user identity.” (source)?
